Most Famous Food Items

Kashmiri Pulao

( J & K Famous)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 inch cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon caraway seeds (jeera)
  • 1 tej patta ((Indian bay leaf))
  • 3 cloves

Sorpotel

( Goa Famous)

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of fatty pork
  • 350 grams pork liver
  • 3 cups finely chopped onions
  • 1 tbsp tamarind pulp
  • 1 tbsp turmeric powder

Aktori

( Himachal Pradesh Famous)

Ingredients

  • 2 cup wheat flour
  • 2 cup buckwheat (kuttu)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup milk

Dhokla

( Gujrat Famous)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Vada-Pav

( Maharashtra Famous)

Ingredients

  • 1 to 1½ cups coriander leaves
  • ½ teaspoon cumin seeds (jeera)
  • 2 hot green chilies (for taste)
  • 2 small garlic cloves (peeled)
  • A teaspoon Lemon juice (taste)

Lassi & Paratha

( Punjab Famous)

Ingredients

  • 2 cup wheat flour
  • ½ tsp salt
  • water (for kneading)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 3 potato / aloo (boiled & mashed)

Idli Sambar

( Tamilnadu Famous)

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup husked and split tuvar dal
  • ¼ cup masoor dal
  • ¼ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 to 2 cups water for cooking
  • 2 tablespoons peanut oil

Bisi-Bele-Bath

( Karnataka Famous)

Ingredients

  • 4 red chilli
  • 1 inch cinnamon (piece)
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal

Dosa Chatani

( Telangana Famous)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • ½ cup poha
  • ½ cup urad dal
  • 2 tablespoons chana dal
  • ½ tsp fenugreek seeds
