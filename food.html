Kashmiri Pulao

( J & K Famous)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 inch cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon caraway seeds (jeera)
  • 1 tej patta ((Indian bay leaf))
  • 3 cloves

Sorpotel

(Goa Famous)

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork
  • 8-10 Kashmiri red chilies
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 8-10 garlic cloves
  • 1 tablespoon cumin seeds

Aktori

(Himachal Pradesh Famous)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup wheat flour
  • 1 cup jaggery
  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1/2 cup ghee

Dhokla

(Gujarat Famous)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 tablespoon semolina (rava)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-green chili paste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Vada-Pav

(Maharashtra Famous)

Ingredients

  • 4 pav bread rolls
  • 4 potato patties (vada)
  • 2 tablespoons green chutney
  • 2 tablespoons tamarind chutney
  • 1/2 cup fried green chilies

Lassi & Paratha

(Punjab Famous)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour

Idli Sambar

(Tamil Nadu Famous)

Bisi Bele Bath
