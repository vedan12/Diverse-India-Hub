BJP

Bharatiya Janata Party: One of the major political parties in India known for its ideology of Hindu nationalism.

Communist Party

Communist Party: A political party advocating socialist principles and ideology.

Aam Aadmi Party

Aam Aadmi Party: A regional political party mainly active in the National Capital Region, known for its anti-corruption stance.

Bahujan Samaj Party

Bahujan Samaj Party: A political party in India that primarily represents the interests of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, and Other Backward Classes.

Samajwadi Party

Samajwadi Party: A regional political party in Uttar Pradesh, known for its socialist and secular policies.

Trinamool Congress

Trinamool Congress: A political party primarily active in the state of West Bengal, known for its regionalism and emphasis on Bengali identity.

Maharashtra Navnirman Sena

Maharashtra Navnirman Sena: A regional political party in Maharashtra, known for its Marathi nationalist ideology.

Shiv Sena

Shiv Sena: A political party primarily active in Maharashtra, known for its pro-Hindutva stance and advocacy for Marathi interests.

Rashtriya Janata Dal

Rashtriya Janata Dal: A political party primarily active in the state of Bihar, known for its advocacy of social justice and backward caste empowerment.
