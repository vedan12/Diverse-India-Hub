Srinagar

The summer capital of Jammu and Kashmir, known for its gardens, waterfronts, and houseboats.

Panaji

The capital of Goa, famous for its beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage.

Shimla

A hill station in Himachal Pradesh, known for its colonial architecture, toy train, and Mall Road.

Ahmedabad

The largest city in Gujarat, famous for its rich culture, textile industry, and Sabarmati Ashram.

Mumbai

The financial capital of India, known for Bollywood, Gateway of India, and Marine Drive.

Amritsar

Home to the Golden Temple, the spiritual and cultural center of Sikhism, and the Wagah Border ceremony.

Chennai

The capital of Tamil Nadu, renowned for its Marina Beach, temples, and classical dance forms.

Bangalore

The Silicon Valley of India, known for its IT industry, gardens, and vibrant nightlife.
