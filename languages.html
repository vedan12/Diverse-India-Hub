Kashmiri

Kashmiri is an Indo-Aryan language primarily spoken in the Kashmir Valley region of Jammu and Kashmir.

Konkani

Konkani is an Indo-Aryan language spoken predominantly in the coastal regions of Goa and parts of Karnataka and Maharashtra.

Hindi

Hindi is one of the official languages of India and is spoken widely across the country, serving as a common language for communication.

Gujarati

Gujarati is an Indo-Aryan language native to the Indian state of Gujarat.

Marathi

Marathi is an Indo-Aryan language spoken predominantly in the Indian state of Maharashtra and neighboring regions.

Punjabi

Punjabi is an Indo-Aryan language spoken by the Punjabi people primarily in the Punjab region of India and Pakistan.

Tamil

Tamil is a Dravidian language spoken predominantly in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu and parts of Sri Lanka, Singapore, and Malaysia.

Kannada

Kannada is a Dravidian language spoken predominantly by the people of Karnataka in southwestern India.

Telugu

Telugu is a Dravidian language spoken primarily in the Indian states of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.
